Summary of Service

Farmmonie is a tech-enabled agricultural platform that provides critical and comprehensive extension support such as but not limited to access to market, funds, inputs for farmers. We use the contributors'("Sponsors") funds to secure farm space, engage farmers, procure inputs, insure farmers and farm produce, complete a full farming cycle, sell harvests and then pay the Sponsor profits from a successfully harvested farming cycle, however if unsuccessful, the Sponsors receive their capital back subject to the insurance coverage. While this farm cycle is on-going, the Sponsors are able to keep track of the full-cycle by getting updates in text, pictures and videos via emails and other media as may be determined by the Company from time to time.
